About Chess Puerto Rico Coffee

Chess Puerto Rico Coffee is a 100% free, forever free online chess platform serving the Puerto Rico community and chess players worldwide. We believe chess should be accessible to everyone — no fees, no premium accounts, no barriers.

♞ Our Mission

To promote chess in Puerto Rico and the Caribbean by providing a world-class, free platform where players of all skill levels can learn, play, and compete. We're building a community around the beautiful game of chess, fueled by great Puerto Rican coffee.

☕ Our Sponsor

Chess Puerto Rico Coffee is proudly sponsored by PuertoRicoCoffeeShop.com — bringing the finest Puerto Rican coffee to chess lovers everywhere. Their generous support makes this platform possible and keeps it free for all players.

🎯 What We Offer

  • Play chess against friends or AI opponents of any difficulty
  • Solve thousands of chess puzzles to sharpen your skills
  • Join tournaments — from casual blitz to serious classical events
  • Analyze your games with powerful Stockfish engine
  • Study openings, endgames, and master games
  • Join a growing community of chess enthusiasts

📍 Based in Puerto Rico

We're based in Isla Verde, Puerto Rico — the heart of the Caribbean. Our platform celebrates Puerto Rican culture while connecting chess players from around the world.
